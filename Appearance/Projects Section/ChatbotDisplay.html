GameBot

Welcome to the Game Recommendation Bot, a Python-based chatbot designed for engaging daily conversations and providing personalized game recommendations. Powered by the ChatterBot library for natural language processing, the bot allows users to enjoy casual interactions and, with a simple command '/game', receive tailored game suggestions based on their preferred genre and gaming platform. Leveraging the RAWG Video Games Database API, the bot ensures up-to-date and relevant recommendations. Whether chatting for fun or seeking the next gaming adventure, this project showcases versatility and user-centric functionality.
