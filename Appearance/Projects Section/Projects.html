Weather Forecast App

Welcome to the Weather Forecast App, a Python-based application designed to provide accurate and personalized weather forecasts for any location. Powered by Streamlit for a user-friendly interface and utilizing the OpenWeatherMap API, this app delivers real-time weather updates, ensuring you are always prepared for the day ahead. Users can easily input their desired location and select the number of forecast days, and get weather updates instantly.

Motion Detector

Welcome to the Motion Detector, an innovative application crafted to enhance your security measures with cutting-edge technology. Powered by Flask and OpenCV, this motion detection system provides real-time alerts whenever movement is detected within its surveillance area. With its intuitive interface and seamless integration, the Motion Detector empowers users to monitor their surroundings effortlessly. Simply activate the detector by entering your email address, and receive instant notifications whenever motion is detected.
