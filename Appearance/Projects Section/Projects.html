Weather Forecast App

Welcome to the Weather Forecast App, a Python-based application designed to provide accurate and personalized weather forecasts for any location. Powered by Streamlit for a user-friendly interface and utilizing the OpenWeatherMap API, this app delivers real-time weather updates, ensuring you are always prepared for the day ahead. Users can easily input their desired location and select the number of forecast days, and get weather updates instantly.

Motion Detector

Welcome to the Motion Detector, an innovative application crafted to enhance your security measures with cutting-edge technology. Powered by Flask and OpenCV, this motion detection system provides real-time alerts whenever movement is detected within its surveillance area.

With its intuitive interface and seamless integration, the Motion Detector empowers users to monitor their surroundings effortlessly. Simply activate the detector by entering your email address, and receive instant notifications whenever motion is detected.

Crypto Price Predictor

Welcome to Crypto Price Predictor, a sophisticated application designed to provide precise and insightful forecasts for cryptocurrency prices. Leveraging advanced machine learning algorithms with a sleek and user-friendly interface, our tool empowers investors and enthusiasts alike to make informed decisions in the volatile world of digital currencies.

Crypto Price Predictor harnesses the power of XGBoost and extensive historical data to deliver reliable predictions. With just a few clicks, users can select their preferred cryptocurrency, set the desired currency pair, and view detailed price forecasts for the next four months. Our application also offers a visual representation of both historical data and future projections, making it easier than ever to track market trends and anticipate future movements.

While the current model provides valuable insights, it may not yet deliver the most accurate results. However, as the model continues to train on more data, its precision will improve, offering even more reliable predictions in the future.
